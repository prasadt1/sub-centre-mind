# Agent guidance — Sub-Centre Mind

Per global defaults in `~/AGENTS.md`; this file **overrides** only what matters here.

## Voice (README, judge-facing prose, ADRs)

Use **first person singular** — **I**, not **we** — when describing what was built, refused, or claimed (e.g. “What I built”, “I do not claim …”). Product or system behaviour may stay third person (“Sub-Centre Mind does …”).

## International readers

Indian institutional terms (**Sub-Centre**, **PHC**, **ASHA**, **NHM**, etc.) should include a **plain-language gloss** and/or a **stable Wikipedia (or official) link** on first substantive mention in README/ADR, so non-Indian judges are not assumed to know the facility pyramid.
